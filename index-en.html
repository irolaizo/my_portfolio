FUZED Borås — Social Media & AI Game Jam

I built FUZED Borås's social media presence from scratch. FUZED is a city-run centre that combines gaming with physical activity. I managed Instagram with original photography (with proper parental permissions), AI-generated graphics edited in Procreate, and professional photography sessions.

During summer 2025 I also ran the ung.boras account, where Stories proved to be the highest-performing format with the target audience. I designed signage and posters for the physical space: opening hours, capacity signs, flyers and internal communications.

The flagship project was the Game Jam — a 12-hour overnight event for young people aged 13–19 where they learned to build video games using AI. I managed the full campaign, secured 10,800 kr in funding, and the event became an official Summer Goal of the programme.
